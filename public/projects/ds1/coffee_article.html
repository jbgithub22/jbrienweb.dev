1 Introduction

This dataset contains daily transaction data from a coffee vending machine from March 2024 to June 2024. In this article, we want to do an exploratory data analysis of the dataset, and make use of each variable identify customer behaviours and trends.

Exploring deeper, we will bring in other datasets which can shed light on the trends and behaviours observed. Thereafter we will conclude by using Machine Learning and ChatGPT to assist us in making predictions for future trends.
